Overview

Indian Intellect is one of the fastest growing Human Resource Company in India which provides end to end HR solutions every employer needs. We have been playing magnificent role in providing both clients and candidates with an unparalleled level of services. Since we are into Human Resource solutions all our actions come with a Human Touch. The sole purpose of this company is to bridge the gap between employers & talented job aspirants and to provide best fit for each other in a transparent manner. Due to dynamic consumer demand and macroeconomic uncertainties, there is a need of flexibility in the industrial value chain. Hence, there is continuous pressure on every organization to be lean and be ready to grab every single opportunity from the market. Flexibility is the only answer to financial viability. Here comes the role of third party or outsourced talented, agile and motivated human resource.

Training and Skill Development

One of the biggest challenge in today’s ever changing work environment is keeping up to date with technology and training workforce to embrace it. A lot of organizations as well as individuals do not grow just because they are unable to adapt themselves to current dynamic business environment.

Indian Intellect not just continuously trains its employees to be relevant in all circumstances but also provides professional trainings to permanent employees of our partner organizations. Companies can even partner with us just for training of their existing workforce.

Apart from different technical skills like Lean Six Sigma, Risk Management, Operational Optimization, various software & hardware training , we also train in soft skills like presentation building, sales pitch creation and workforce handling.
